UX SKILLS

As a UX/UI designer, I’m passionate about creating meaningful and intuitive experiences rooted in a deep understanding of users. By combining thorough research, empathy, and qualitative interviews, I aim to uncover real user needs and behaviors. This allows me to design solutions that not only solve problems but also resonate personally with users, making their interactions seamless and enjoyable."

USER RESEARCH

I focus on building real connections with users during research. Through open-ended interviews, I gain deep insights that help me create solutions that feel natural and meet users' actual needs.

USER EMPATHY

I truly care about understanding the people I design for. By listening to their stories and watching how they interact, I uncover what really matters to them. This lets me create designs that not only solve problems but also feel personal and meaningful.

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

I believe in the power of conversation. By using insights from qualitative interviews throughout the design process, I make sure the solutions I create reflect what users genuinely want. The result is a product that’s both useful and enjoyable to use.
